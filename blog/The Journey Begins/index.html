Color, mood, and small decisions

Jan 2025 · Visuals, UI

Sometimes the biggest change in how a site feels comes from tiny, nearly invisible shifts in color. Not a full rebrand — just a nudge of saturation or warmth.

This site started with slightly cooler neutrals. Pushing them warmer made the whole space feel more like a study than a dashboard.

I like thinking of color in terms of mood more than hex codes: “quiet”, “wooden”, “noisy”, “electric”. The hexes come later.
